Felix Y. Manalo Foundation

About "The Felix Y. Manalo (FYM) Foundation, Inc. is a non-stock, non-profit, and non-political organization. It is organized under the Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law for public and charitable purposes."
Mission "Provide opportunity and equity for those most in need. Promote education, socio-economic well-being, environmental awareness and health improvement for everyone. Contribute to the creation of peaceful and productive communities, not only in the United States, but also in other countries."
Notable Events & Projects
  • November 2012: International Aid to Humanity - Hurricane Sandy Relief - New York City, United States
  • February 2013: International Aid to Humanity - Cyclone Oswald Relief - Queensland, Australia
  • April 2013: My Countrymen, My Brethren - Poverty Outreach - Pasay City, Philippines
  • May 2013: International Aid to Humanity - EF5 Tornado Relief - Oklahoma, United States
  • January 2016: International Aid to Humanity - Poverty Outreach - Taguig City, Philippines
  • May 2016: International Aid to Humanity - Wildfire Relief - Alberta, Canada
  • May 2016: Nationwide Blood Donation and Bone Marrow Registry Drive - United States & Canada
  • April 2017: International Aid to Humanity - Poverty Outreach - Zambia, Africa
  • May 2017: Nationwide Blood Donation Drive - United States
Aside from these large-scale events, the FYM Foundation also conducts eco-farming projects in impoverished communities to create self-sustainable jobs and resources for members of those communities.
